Welcome to the internet home of Albuquerque’s resource for locally produced lumber!

We turn cut trees into valuable wood products, generating a sustainable resource while reducing the local waste stream.

Whether you have a tree you want milled and dried, or are looking for a quality source of locally procured and processed hardwood materials, you have come to the right place.

If you have a wood project in mind, and need to be connected with artisans and craftspeople to make it happen, we can help you with that as well.

Finally, if you have questions or needs regarding any stage of a tree’s growth, maintenance, or removal, we can get you to the right information and resources.

We would love to hear from you.
